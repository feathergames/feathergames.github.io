Antimicrobial Side-Effects

AntibioticSide Effects/Further info
Macrolides (-omycin)Prolongs QT. GI side effects. P450 inhibitor, stop statins when used. Azithromycin can cause hearing loss and tinnitus
Quinolones (-floxacin)Lower seizure threshold. Tendon damage. Prolongs QT. Avoid in pregnant/breastfeeding/G6PD
TB - RifampicinPotent liver enzyme inducer. Hepatitis, Orange secretions. Prevents transcription of DNA to mRNA.
TB - IsoniazidPeripheral neuropathy (add pyridoxine - vit B6). Hepatitis. Agranulocytosis. Inhibit mycolic acid synthesis.
TB - PyrazinamideHyperuricaemia, gout. Hepatitis. Turns into pyrazinoic acid which inhibits FAS (fatty acid synthas)
TB - EthambutolOptic neuritis - check visual acuity before and during treatment. Inhibit arabinan synthesis.
